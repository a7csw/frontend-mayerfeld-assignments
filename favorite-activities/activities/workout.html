Weekly Workout & Calisthenics Training

My dedication to physical fitness and strength training

My Fitness Journey

Physical fitness has always been an important part of my life. I believe that a strong body supports a strong mind, and my weekly workout routine reflects this philosophy.

I focus on calisthenics training, which uses bodyweight exercises to build strength, flexibility, and endurance. This approach has helped me develop functional strength that translates to real-world activities.

In addition to calisthenics, I also practice armwrestling, which has taught me about leverage, technique, and mental toughness.

Weekly Training Schedule

💪

Monday - Upper Body

Push-ups, pull-ups, dips, and shoulder exercises focusing on chest, back, and arms.

🦵

Wednesday - Lower Body

Squats, lunges, calf raises, and core exercises for leg strength and stability.

🤝

Friday - Armwrestling

Technique practice, grip training, and competitive matches with training partners.

🧘

Sunday - Recovery

Light stretching, mobility work, and active recovery to prepare for the week ahead.

Benefits of My Training

💪

Functional Strength

Building real-world strength that helps with daily activities and sports performance.

🧠

Mental Discipline

Developing focus, perseverance, and mental toughness through consistent training.

⚡

Energy & Vitality

Increased energy levels and overall vitality that supports my academic and personal life.

🎯

Goal Achievement

Setting and achieving fitness goals builds confidence and transferable life skills.
