Weekly Workout & Calisthenics Training

Building strength, discipline, and resilience through bodyweight training

My Fitness Philosophy

Staying physically fit is not just about looking good - it's about feeling strong, maintaining discipline, and building the mental resilience that carries over into every aspect of life. My weekly workout routine centers around calisthenics training and armwrestling practice, both of which have become integral parts of my identity.

I believe in the power of bodyweight exercises and functional strength. Calisthenics teaches you to master your own body weight, building not just muscle but also coordination, flexibility, and control. Combined with armwrestling, which demands explosive power and technique, my training regimen keeps me challenged and motivated.

Why I Love This Routine

Benefits of My Training

  • Builds Functional Strength: Every exercise translates to real-world movement patterns
  • Develops Mental Toughness: Pushing through challenging workouts builds character
  • Improves Focus: Physical training enhances my ability to concentrate on studies
  • Stress Relief: Exercise is my go-to method for managing academic pressure
  • Consistent Progress: Weekly routine ensures steady improvement and habit formation

The Armwrestling Connection

Armwrestling has taught me that strength isn't just about size - it's about technique, leverage, and mental strategy. The sport demands explosive power, grip strength, and the ability to adapt tactics mid-match. These skills have made me more strategic in my approach to both fitness and life challenges.
